Goalsreach

Turn Productivity Into Rewards

Why I Created Goalsreach

I built Goalsreach because I saw how easily we indulge in distractions—whether it’s gaming, scrolling, or partying—without achieving what truly matters. I wanted a system that ties enjoyment to progress. Goalsreach ensures you earn your indulgences through productivity, making fun more rewarding and guilt-free.

How It Works

1. Set Your Goals

Create goals, break them into subgoals, and assign points based on their difficulty.

2. Earn Accountability Points

Complete goals and subgoals to earn points that reflect your progress.

3. Create Your Rewards

Set rewards for yourself, from movies to nights out, with a point cost tied to them.

4. Enjoy What You Earn

Spend points to indulge in your rewards guilt-free, knowing you've earned them.

Key Features

Goal Management

Break down big tasks into subgoals and track progress visually.

Accountability Points

Earn points for completing goals and see your progress grow in real time.

Custom Rewards

Design personal rewards to keep yourself motivated and productive.

Progress Visualisation

Stay motivated with streaks, charts, and progress bars.
