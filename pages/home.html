About Me

Background

I’m an iOS developer and product-focused designer with a First-Class Honours BSc in Digital Media from UWE Bristol. My work sits where product design and engineering overlap — taking early ideas and shaping them into simple, intuitive tools people actually use.

Products I’m Building

Goalsreach is a self-built productivity app designed to help people turn intentions into consistent action. Using gamification, habit loops, and clean UX, it makes discipline feel rewarding rather than draining. Live on the iOS App Store.

BrandCore is an AI-powered branding platform for early founders — generating brand identity, messaging, and first product directions using OpenAI APIs. It’s the tool I wish I had when I started building products at seventeen.

How I Work

My approach is simple: tight build–measure loops, clean interaction flows, and a bias towards shipping. I work end-to-end — from research and UX to SwiftUI implementation — ensuring the final product feels cohesive rather than stitched together in parts.

What Matters To Me

I’m driven by clarity, discipline, and long-term thinking. The products I build are rooted in a simple aim — helping people gain more control, momentum, and confidence in their own lives. That philosophy shapes my work, my training, and the way I show up professionally: calm, steady, and focused on what genuinely moves people forward.
